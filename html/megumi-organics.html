Megumi Organics

↪ Project Site

Tools: Shopify, HTML, CSS, JavaScript

Megumi Organics creates products made from 100% organic plants—no fillers, shortcuts, or half measures needed.

When Lisa Megumi transitioned her business from a Hawaii-based organic hair salon to a global e-commerce site, she wanted a new, digital space for her clients to shop her products with ease while maintaing the personal connection she has with them.

My challenge was to design a branding strategy that translates the natural and clean energy of Lisa's physical salon into the digital nature of the web, while building out a website that increase sales.
