Halo Halo
HTML, CSS, three.js

Welcome to Halo-Halo, a 3D-rendered website that pays homage to the emblematic Filipino dessert, the halo-halo. Immerse yourself in a delightful journey that takes you deep into the heart of this beloved treat.

Using a combination of HTML, CSS, and Three.js, each navigational page offers an exploration of the dessert's three main layers of ingredients.

As you navigate through the website, you'll embark on an immersive experience that mirrors the joy of indulging in a real halo halo. Each layer represents a distinct layer of the dessert, crafted as 3D models using the power of Three.js. From the refreshing crushed ice to the colorful assortment of fruits, beans, and jellies, every detail is brought to life three dimensionally.
