Mecha Mecca

↪ Project Site

Tools: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP

Mecha Mecca is an in immersive marketplace for your digital garden. This site is developed and designed as if it were a shopping site inside a game.

In order to access the shop, the user must register or login to their account. Once inside the shop, the user may add any of the 6 available plants for sale to their shopping cart. Once they press 'CHECKOUT,' the total price will be calculated and they may enter their shipping info. A receipt will then be outputted. Mecha Mecca also includes an 'About' page, search bar, and contact page.

Each of the 6 digital plants featured on Mecha Mecca were created by 3D Artist Kristin Kweon.
