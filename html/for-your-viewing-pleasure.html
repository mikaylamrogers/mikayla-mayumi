Welcome to "For Your Viewing Pleasure," an interactive web project that serves as a comprehensive film directory showcasing my favorite movies from the quarantine era of 2020.

Designed and developed using a combination of HTML, CSS, and jQuery, this platform offers a visually engaging experience for film enthusiasts.

As you explore the directory, you'll notice that each film title comes to life when you hover over it.
