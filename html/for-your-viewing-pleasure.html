"For Your Viewing Pleasure"
HTML, CSS, jQuery

Welcome to "For Your Viewing Pleasure," a captivating and interactive web project that serves as a comprehensive film directory showcasing my favorite movies from the quarantine era of 2020.

Designed and developed using a combination of HTML, CSS, and jQuery, this platform offers a visually engaging experience for film enthusiasts like you. Immerse yourself in a collection of remarkable movies that have left a lasting impression during these unique times.

As you explore the directory, you'll notice that each film title comes to life when you hover over it. Witness the magic unfold as a still from the respective movie is elegantly displayed, offering a tantalizing glimpse into its captivating visuals. This feature adds an element of intrigue and entices you to delve deeper into the world of each film.
